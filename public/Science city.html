Science City, Kolkata

History

Science City in Kolkata, inaugurated on July 1, 1997, is one of the largest science centers in the Indian subcontinent. Managed by the National Council of Science Museums, it offers a perfect blend of education and entertainment. The complex features a range of interactive science exhibits, including the Space Odyssey, Dynamotion Hall, and Earth Exploration Hall. It was developed to promote scientific awareness among people of all age groups and is a popular destination for both students and tourists.

Location

Science City is located at EM Bypass, J.B.S. Haldane Avenue, Kolkata, West Bengal – easily accessible from various parts of the city.

Places to Visit Nearby

  • Eco Park – A vast green space with recreational activities, boating, and gardens.
  • Nicco Park – A popular amusement park for families and kids.
  • Birla Temple – A stunning white marble Hindu temple located nearby.
  • Acropolis Mall – A modern shopping mall with food courts and cinemas.
  • Kolkata Museum of Modern Art (Upcoming) – Located in Rajarhat, just a short drive away.
