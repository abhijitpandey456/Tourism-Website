Park Street, Kolkata

Overview

Park Street, officially known as Mother Teresa Sarani, is one of the most iconic roads in Kolkata, West Bengal. Known for its vibrant nightlife, colonial-era architecture, and historical importance, Park Street has been the heart of the city's cultural and entertainment life for decades. It's lined with some of the most famous restaurants, cafes, and pubs in Kolkata.

Location

Park Street is located in central Kolkata and connects Chowringhee Road to Park Circus. It is easily accessible via metro, bus, and taxi.

Famous Spots on Park Street

  • Flurys – An iconic bakery and tearoom founded in 1927
  • Peter Cat – Famous for its Chelo Kebab and retro ambiance
  • Park Hotel – A luxury hotel and nightlife hub
  • Oxford Bookstore – A paradise for book lovers
  • Someplace Else – A popular pub for live music and drinks
